DOT Box Truck Driver — Tracy, CA

Full-time • Non‑CDL • Dock‑to‑dock

Job description

This job consists of a person driving a NON-CDL box truck and driving dock to dock. All trucks come with an electric lift gate and industrial hand truck/dolly. We start early and finish early- great for those who enjoy being home early. Must be polite and professional, customer satisfaction is a must.

Requirements

  • DOT MED CARD IS REQUIRED.
  • NO CDL required (Regular Class C License is ok)
  • No car Accidents last 3 Years
  • Load box trucks.
  • Shift starts between 10 AM- 2 AM, 7- 9 hour shift.
  • One weekend day a week is required.
  • Follow strict safety standards on and off the road
  • Operate an electronic device for GPS and daily records
  • 23+ Years old
  • 1+ Year of box truck experience required
  • Excellent Attendance Record
  • Dedication + Willingness to Learn
  • Excellent driving history
  • Adhere to a Full Background Check
