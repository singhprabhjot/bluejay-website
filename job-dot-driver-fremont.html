DOT Box Truck Driver — Fremont

Fremont, CA • Full-time

Role Overview

Operate 16'–26' non‑CDL box trucks for dock‑to‑dock and last‑mile routes. Maintain DOT compliance, follow optimized routes, and provide exceptional customer service.

Compensation & Schedule

  • $25.50/hour starting pay
  • Shifts typically begin at 10:00 AM
  • Full‑time; at least one weekend day requested
  • Benefits may include PTO, health insurance, bonuses, paid training, and advancement paths

Qualifications

  • 23+ years old; valid driver’s license (3+ years); no CDL required
  • DOT Medical Card; acceptable MVR/history per policy
  • Able to enter/exit vehicle frequently; lift 75 lbs solo / 300 lbs with team
  • Background check and drug screening
  • Smartphone/GPS proficiency
