Helpers

Fremont, CA • Full-time / Part-time

Role Overview

Assist drivers with loading/unloading and white‑glove services (installations, recycling, room‑of‑choice, photo confirmation). Provide excellent customer service and follow safety protocols.

Compensation & Schedule

  • Starting at $20.00/hour; full‑time and part‑time roles
  • Shifts typically begin at 10:00 AM; at least one weekend day requested
  • Benefits may include PTO, health insurance, overtime as needed, bonuses, advancement opportunities, and shoe credit

Qualifications

  • 18+ years old; valid state ID
  • Comfort repeatedly entering/exiting vehicles and using stairs
  • Lift up to 90 lbs solo and up to 300 lbs with team lift
  • Willing to complete background check and drug screening
  • Reliable, safety‑minded, and customer‑focused
