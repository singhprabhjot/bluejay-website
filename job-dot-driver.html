DOT Box Truck Driver — Tracy

Tracy, CA • Full-time

Role Overview

Operate non‑CDL box trucks for dock‑to‑dock routes with an electric liftgate and hand truck/dolly. Safety, professionalism, and customer satisfaction are a must.

Compensation & Schedule

  • $25.75/hour starting pay
  • Shift starts between 10:00 PM – 2:00 AM; typical 7–9 hour shift
  • One weekend day per week required
  • Benefits may include 401(k), health/dental/vision, PTO, paid training, flexible schedule

Qualifications

  • 23+ years old; valid Class C; DOT Medical Card required
  • 100% clean driving record; no accidents in last 3 years
  • 1+ year box‑truck experience
  • Operate smartphone/GPS; excellent attendance; pass background check
