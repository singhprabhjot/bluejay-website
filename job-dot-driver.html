DOT Box Truck Driver — Tracy

Tracy, CA • Full-time

Job Description

This position involves driving a non‑CDL box truck dock‑to‑dock. Trucks are equipped with an electric lift gate and industrial hand truck/dolly. Drivers start early and finish early, ideal for those who enjoy being home sooner. Professionalism and customer satisfaction are essential.

Key Responsibilities

  • Drive non‑CDL box trucks safely on dock‑to‑dock routes
  • Load and unload trucks with liftgate and hand truck/dolly
  • Follow strict safety standards on and off the road
  • Operate GPS and electronic logging devices
  • Maintain excellent attendance and professionalism

Requirements

  • DOT Medical Card required
  • Regular Class C License (no CDL required)
  • No accidents in the last 3 years
  • 23+ years old
  • 1+ year box truck driving experience
  • Excellent driving record and willingness to learn
  • Pass full background check

Schedule

  • Shift start between 10:00 AM – 2:00 AM
  • Typical shift length: 7–10 hours
  • One weekend day required
  • Night shifts possible

Compensation

Pay: From $25.75/hour

Benefits

  • 401(k) and 401(k) matching
  • Health, dental, and vision insurance
  • Company truck
  • Flexible schedule
  • Paid time off and paid training
  • Referral program
