Van Driver

Fremont, CA • Full-time / Part-time

Role Overview

Deliver small‑parcel routes safely and efficiently using company‑provided, front‑wheel‑drive vans. Follow GPS‑optimized routes and provide excellent customer service.

Compensation & Schedule

  • From $24.50/hour (8–10 hour shifts)
  • Typical shift: 10:00 AM to route completion
  • Full‑time: 4–5 days/week; Part‑time: 2–3 days/week
  • Benefits may include health/dental/vision, PTO (up to 80 hours/year), 401(k), paid training

Qualifications

  • 21+ years old; valid driver’s license, clean MVR (3+ years licensed)
  • Lift 75 lbs solo / 300 lbs with team; frequent in/out of vehicle and stairs
  • Background check and drug screening
